In a nutshell

I design in diverse, interdisciplinary ways - mostly UX/UI/LX.

Currently, I work on "The Creativity Canvas" ,

Project

The creativity canvas is a blueprint for individuals and small teams, to explore and understand their creativity.

The canvas introduces 16 elements clustered in 5 categories that are central to understand creativity.

It is based on the Business Model Canvas and various theories on creativity.

Unline the Business Model Canvas, the Creativity Canvas invites people to think beyond the proposed framework which offers a starting point only.
