Concept design for an ecommerce solution for a local art store (exercise).

Project

Joia was a project within my UX/UI bootcamp.

This ecommerce concept is designed to reflect the artsy shop experience of a portugese store that sells prints, illustrations and crafty postcards.

Interviews and surveys showed that - after struggling to find these kind of shops - mostly value the atmosphere. The website therefore simulates a store visit rather then the otherwise known ecommerce design.

The major elements are illustrations and a gallery-like user flow. The second was developed and tested throughout low-, mid- and high-level prototypes.

Info

Scope

  • Real world project
  • Low/Mid/High fedelity prototypes 2 weeks
  • Prototype testing

Tools & Techniques

  • UX Research: Quantiative Research, Interviews, Observation
  • Insight Generation: Persona, Affinity Diagram, Concept Generation / Testing
  • Prototype: Figma, Adobe Illustrator
