Creative You! A-Z concept, design and prototype for a creativity exploration app as final project.

Project

Creative You! is the outcome of my UX/UI bootcamp's final project.

The app targests users that want to explore, understand and develop their creativity. It also allows to share your reflections on your creativity and creative process with others.

Based on "The Creativity Canvas" (a creativity framework I developed), you first answer prompts that you store in zines. You also can build your own creativity model that you, too, capture in shareable zines.

Gamification elements through badges and a strong narrative through the app's avatar "Rob" help you to stay on track. The social sharing element provides another incentive to invest time to design your creativity.
